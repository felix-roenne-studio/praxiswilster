# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two groups, roughly equal weight:

- **Neue Patientinnen**: Women in and around Wilster searching for a gynaecologist (often via Google Maps or search). Primary job: confirm the practice takes new patients, find the phone number, and call to book. They have low patience for friction; if the phone number is not visible within seconds, they move on.
- **Bestandspatientinnen**: Existing patients returning for a quick lookup: opening hours, address before an appointment, which services are offered. They know the practice; they need the information fast.

Both groups are primarily on mobile, often mid-errand or in transit. They are not reading; they are scanning for one specific piece of information.

## Product Purpose

A practice information website for Dr. med. Mona Wendrich-Rönne, Fachärztin für Gynäkologie und Geburtshilfe, Rathausstr. 2, 25554 Wilster. The site exists to surface the practice's contact details, opening hours, and service scope so that prospective and existing patients can reach the practice with minimum friction.

Success: a visitor finds the phone number and calls, or confirms an appointment detail without needing to call.

## Brand Personality

**Klar · Kompetent · Nah**

A practice that earns trust through precision and directness, not through emotional warmth or wellness aesthetics. Patients trust it because it is clear about what it offers and does not dress medical information in metaphor. "Nah" (approachable) shows up in copy directness and accessible information architecture, not in soft color or rounded-everything UI.

## Anti-references

- **Esoterisch / ganzheitlich-warm**: Soft gradient backgrounds, pastel colour washes, emotional taglines ("your journey to wellbeing"), spa-retreat visual language, overlapping blurred shapes. This is the practice's explicit anti-aesthetic.
- **Generische Arztpraxis-Website**: Stock-photo doctor in a white coat, DocCheck template blue-and-white, icon rows with generic medical symbols. Blends into every other practice site.
- **Wellness-App visual language**: Figtree-on-mint, large rounded cards, animated blob backgrounds, "take care of yourself" copy cadence.

## Design Principles

1. **Nummer zuerst**: The phone number is the single most important piece of information on the site. It should be the loudest, most immediately scannable element on any viewport.
2. **Kompetenz durch Klarheit**: Medical expertise is demonstrated through precise, structured information, not through emotional copy. Tables beat prose. Lists beat paragraphs.
3. **Kein Zierat ohne Funktion**: Every visual element must serve a patient task. Decoration that does not help a user find information faster is removed.
4. **Nicht-esotherisch bis in die Typografie**: The tone is clinical in a good sense: measured, exact, unambiguous. Copy uses factual nouns and verbs ("Krebsvorsorge", "HPV-Impfung", "3D/4D-Ultraschall"), not category filler ("ganzheitlich", "einfühlsam", "Lebensphase").
5. **Schnell laden, sofort lesbar**: Patients on mobile with spotty rural signal. No heavy assets without a clear return. Perceived performance IS brand quality for a practice site.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG compliance target. Reasonable accessibility as default: adequate text contrast (WCAG AA as a floor), keyboard navigation, semantic HTML, meaningful alt text, minimum 44px touch targets. Reduced motion respected.
